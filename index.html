Bio

Thank you for reviewing my portfolio.

Most of my career comes from an Administrative Assistant and Sales background. This has given me an eye for detail and I am able to coordinate multiple projects at a time.

I transitioned from sales into the IT side of business a couple years ago. This opened my eyes to a whole new world that I wanted to learn and know more about. My role as an IT Coordinator was to communicate between the company I worked for and the 3rd party company that handled their network structure.

I walked into the position of IT Coordinator and did not understand the fundamentals of networking technologies. Over the course of a year I was able facilitate and mange multiple infastructur updates and streamline information properly. Going into my second year in that position, I yearned to understand how programs worked and how they 'talk' to each other. I enrolled in Rice's Coding Bootcamp to gain front end and back end coding knowledge.

In the middle of the bootcamp, not only did I decide to make a career change but decided to move to a new city. My family relocated to New Braunfels, TX in order to be closer to my in-laws.

Completing the bootcamp program, I am amazed at the amount if information I have been able to obtain and utilize. I look forward to learning more and working on more projects.

You can do anything as long as you have inspiration and are driven to succeed
